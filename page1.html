I'm student at LUCA School of Arts in Ghent, Belgium. I study sculpture and I'm very interested in figurative artwork. My works are mostly made with clay, focusing on the bust and body. Afterwards with plaster, ceramics, metal, cast stone, ...

My drawings and paintings are more like studies and sketches of what I see and feel.


The balance of the human head. What is happening on the inside and how do you interpret this in your art? How many emotions can you show? How you portray a person's head is what keeps me busy. 

You just show the outside, the inside or is it your personal emotions as artist that are automatically shown because you made it with your own hands.


A sculpture or just a chunk of clay. What do you think?
